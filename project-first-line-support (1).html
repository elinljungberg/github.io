First Line Support Tool / 2024

Support Smartness

An internal AI-powered tool that helps customer support agents resolve cases faster, built through close collaboration with agents, data scientists, and a deep commitment to inclusive, user-centered design.

Year

2024

Role

UX Research & Design

Tools

Figma, User Interviews, Usability Testing

Background

Customer support agents were relying on a third-party service with limited customization options, making it slow and inefficient to look up relevant information and reach conclusions on cases. As the volume and complexity of support tickets grew, the need for a purpose-built internal tool became clear.

The goal was to replace this external dependency with something tailored entirely to our agents' real workflows, a tool that could leverage historical case data and query the codebase relevant to each incoming case to surface smarter, faster suggestions.

My Role

I led the UX research, usability efforts, and all UI design throughout the project, working closely with diverse user groups to ensure the product met real needs and was inclusive, accessible, and easy to navigate. I was embedded with the team from early discovery through to launch, continuously bringing the agents' perspective into design decisions.

Process

I spent significant time talking directly with customer support agents, observing their daily work, understanding their pain points, and mapping the moments where the old tool let them down.

A key part of the work was collaborating tightly with the data scientists building the model behind the tool. The AI component draws on historical support cases to suggest conclusions and can query the codebase to help agents reason through complex issues. Translating these technical capabilities into something agents could actually trust and act on was a core design challenge.

Usability testing was iterative and inclusive. I worked with agents across different experience levels and backgrounds to make sure the interface worked for everyone.

Outcome

The tool replaced the previous third-party service entirely, giving agents a significantly more capable and customizable environment. With AI-powered suggestions surfaced directly in their workflow, agents can reach conclusions faster and with greater confidence. The close collaboration between design, agents, and data science resulted in a product that feels genuinely useful.
